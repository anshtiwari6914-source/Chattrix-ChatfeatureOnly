Getting Started

Click **Start Chat** and you’ll be matched with a random user instantly. No Anxiety needed — just your camera, mic, and curiosity.

  • Make sure your camera & mic permissions are allowed.
  • Use a stable internet connection for smoother calls.
  • Click **Skip** anytime to talk to someone new.

Common Issues

Camera Not Working

Check browser permissions → Ensure “Camera: Allow”.

Mic Not Working

Test your microphone in your device settings or another app.

Can’t Find Anyone

Traffic may be low. Try again — or refresh the page.

How to Report Someone

Tap **Report** → Select reason → Submit. We review all reports carefully.
